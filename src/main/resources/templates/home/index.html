Manage and share your own Technology Radar
If you don't pay attention to changing technology you can quickly find yourself falling behind the industry. We all need to pay attention to changes and continuously evaluate those changes to see if industry changes help or hurt us. A technology radar lets you keep track of your assessments of the risks and rewards of various areas of technology, and by joining this site you can manage your own technology radar.

Have multiple versions
Your opinion of things can and should change over time. Something that was the defacto standard once upon a time has more than likely been kicked to the curb by newer ways of doing things. Keep track of how you feel about things over time and be able to look at that history when you need to remember why you chose something.
See what others think
As other people enter in their own evaluations of things you'll be able to see what they have learned about the same technologies you are considering.
Show others your radar
You're radar can be published to be visible to anyone on the internet, or kept private if you just want to keep it for yourself.
Let others update your radar
COMING SOON! We rarely work individually and often we want to manage a teams worth of decisions. Here you can grant access that allows anyone on your team to add or update items on your radar.
